Sgt. Pepper's Lonely Hearts Club Band

Musicians:

Sgt. Pepper's Lonely Hearts Club Band is the eighth studio album by the English rock band the Beatles. Released on 26 May 1967, Sgt. Pepper is regarded by musicologists as an early concept album that advanced the roles of sound composition, extended form, psychedelic imagery, record sleeves, and the producer in popular music.

Magical Mystery Tour

Released Dec 8 1967

Musicians:

  • John Lennon
  • Paul McCartney
  • George Harrison
  • Ringo Starr

Magical Mystery Tour is an double EP by The Beatles, both including the six-song soundtrack to the 1967 film of the same name. The record format released in December 1967, was a six-track double EP on the Parlophone label.

Side One

  • Magical Mystery Tour
  • Your Mother Should Know
  • I am the Walrus

Side Two

  • The Fool on the Hill
  • Flying
  • Blue Jay Way
  • Shooting proceeded on the basis of a mostly handwritten collection of ideas, sketches and situations. Magical Mystery Tour was ultimately the shortest of all Beatles films, although almost ten hours of footage was shot over a two-week period.
  • Magical Mystery Tour the movie was a made-for-television musical film directed by and starring the Beatles.
  • The Magical Mystery Tour rough take was only 10 hours.
  • When recording their new songs, the Beatles continued the studio experimentation that had typified Sgt. Pepper's Lonely Hearts Club Band (1967)
